TRIVIA - Cats

How to play TRIVIA - THE GAME

  1. You have to answer 10 questions. Each question has three options.

  2. For every correct answer, you are going to get 10 points.

  3. For every correct answer, you also can see green marks and red for incorrect.
  4. After final answer, you can see the total points and correct answers.
  5. In the end you can choose to play again or to quit.
